What does Atom computation do?

Display Librairie

Java librairie inspired from MeanRollerCoding graphical engine. The mechanics are similar to swing and awt with a boost in modularity. The goal here is to offer a simple, but quite powerful, alternative to those two java librairies. From this unique librairie, you could produce form and user interface.
